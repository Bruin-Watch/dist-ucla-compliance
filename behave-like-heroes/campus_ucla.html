Body-Worn Cameras (BWC) UCLA PD implemented body-worn cameras on January 1, 2021 for all active sworn personnel (approximately 51 officers at the time), following a UC systemwide BWC policy (Chapter 15 of UC Universitywide Police Policies and Administrative Procedures). RIPA / Racial Stop Data UCLA PD began RIPA (Racial and Identity Profiling Act, AB 953) data collection in January 2022 (Wave 4/5 agencies). Mandatory Disclosure Era  — Events On or After January 1, 2019 (SB 1421 Effective Date)
January 1, 2019 SB 1421 TAKES EFFECT — MANDATORY DISCLOSURE OF OFFICER MISCONDUCT RECORDS. Senate Bill 1421 (CA Penal Code §832.7(b)) takes effect statewide. As of this date, all California law enforcement agencies — including UCLA PD — are required to disclose records related to: (1) officer use of force resulting in death or great bodily injury; (2) sexual assault by officers; and (3) sustained findings of dishonesty.
